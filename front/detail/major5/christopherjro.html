Chris Ro is a Korean American graphic designer based in Seoul, South Korea. He currently teaches graphic design and typography at Hongik University and runs his own independent practice exploring both commercial and cultural projects. Chris served as chief curator of the Typojanchi 2015: The 4thInternational Typography Biennale. From 2013 to 2016 Chris was a board member of the Korean Society of Typography. He is currently a member of the Alliance Graphique Internationale.
